## §2 · Reject section · tempting-but-wrong directions

Three (and a fourth) directions that look attractive at first glance and should NOT be built now.

### Reject R1 · A second corporate-advisory firm in a different palette
**Why tempting**: easy to author, fits LF-1 verbatim, palette + voice differentiation feels sufficient.
**Why wrong**: LF-1 is occupied (Pragma) and CS-LAYOUT-11 forbids two siblings sharing a layout family. A "different-palette Pragma" would either ship LF-1 (forbidden) or ship a 55/45 split with KPI tuple at LF-{NEW} (collides with Pragma at 5/9 layout · Fiscus near-occupancy ladder forces a § decision review). The cluster's structural weak link (Pragma↔Fiscus 2/9) was just formally accepted; adding a third near-occupant at sibling 6 invalidates the single-exception ladder per `distinctness-matrix.md §4`.

### Reject R2 · A second commercialista (Wave-2 specialist · e.g., revisione legale-only or commercialista internazionale)
**Why tempting**: market exists, fits LF-3 verbatim, distinctness via copy.
**Why wrong**: same CS-LAYOUT-11 lock — LF-3 is occupied (Fiscus). A second commercialista would inherit the slot-4 fiscal-cycle (Fiscus's family signature) or strip it (no longer LF-3). Both paths fail the gate. Commercialista is one professional fit; the cluster does not need two siblings serving it.

### Reject R3 · A SaaS founder / digital-product studio
**Why tempting**: largest TAM, highest commercial pull, "modern + premium" reads natively in the vertical.
**Why wrong**: corporate-suite is explicitly **not SaaS**. CS-MARKET-01 + CS-MARKET-04 + the CTA contract (CS-CTA-02 · "Get started free" / "Sign up now" banned) all gate against this. SaaS demands product-led structure (pricing-page · feature-grid · trial-CTA · social-proof-by-logos) that the cluster's editorial restraint refuses. A "premium digital studio" would either drift the cluster into SaaS visual language (collapses the brand) or be authored to clear cluster gates and end up reading as another advisory firm (collides with Pragma).

### Reject R4 · A premium ecommerce or hospitality brand
**Why tempting**: bridges to other archetypes already in the catalog (Bottega/Luxe/Sapore/Brace etc.) and could reuse imagery curation infrastructure.
**Why wrong**: archetype boundary. The corporate-suite is **professional services** (advisory · architecture · commercialista · coaching · stewardship · legal · audit · notary · …). Ecommerce/hospitality lives in different archetypes (`business-ecommerce` / `business-restaurant`) with different proof shapes and CTA contracts. A 6th corporate-suite sibling that crosses the archetype boundary breaks the cluster's editorial isolation.

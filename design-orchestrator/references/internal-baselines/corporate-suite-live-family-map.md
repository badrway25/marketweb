## §4 · Open territory rules for the next candidate

The 6th sibling chooses one of: LF-2 second occupant · LF-6 first occupant · LF-{NEW}. The rules differ.

### LF-2 second occupant
**Allowed only if** the sibling is a portfolio-of-work-led firm whose proof IS its case studies (evidence-led litigation · independent directorship case-bundle · audit-led methodology with published methodology pieces · longitudinal research practice with publications).
**Required**: read `cornice-lf2-reference-pack.md` first. The pack lists the load-bearing differentiators that Cornice owns (curatorial-architectural register · `argomento` voice anchor · cream-paper navbar · stacked-editorial hero · single-portrait masthead · 3+1 magazine grid). The second LF-2 occupant inherits the L1–L9 tuple verbatim and differentiates inside the cells — different voice anchor, different palette, different hero photography subject, different leadership composition (e.g., 2-portrait diptych instead of single-portrait).
**Hard limits**: cannot collide with Cornice on (a) palette family (graphite + pietra-serena + rust is taken), (b) Cormorant Garamond as heading serif, (c) Bologna or any northern-Italian-portico exterior as hero subject, (d) `argomento` or any direct cognate as voice anchor noun, (e) "Apri un fascicolo progetto" or the "fascicolo" mental model as CTA. The second LF-2 occupant scores against Cornice's column at the matrix re-fresh (see §6).

### LF-6 first occupant
**Allowed for**: magazine-edited boutiques (audit-led methodology · public-hearing law · conservation studios · longitudinal-research practices).
**Required**: file an addendum to `corporate-suite-layout-family-matrix.md §1` finalising the LF-6 row. The L1–L9 tuple is provisional today (`side-rail-photo · E · slot-6 · side-quote-with-cards · band-at-4 · photo-grid · collage-3+1 · side-rail · condensed-single-row`) — the first occupant binds it.
**Hard limit**: a sibling that picks LF-6 cannot reuse LF-2's editorial-publication register on the chrome (cream-paper masthead is Cornice's). LF-6's "magazine" gravity must come from a different mechanism — likely the side-rail nav itself.

### LF-{NEW}
**Allowed for**: any candidate that does not fit LF-1..LF-6.
**Required**: file an addendum declaring the new L1–L9 tuple **before build**. The tuple must score ≥4/9 different vs every existing column (LF-1, LF-2, LF-3, LF-4, LF-5) and ≥4/9 vs LF-6 if the planner intends LF-6 to remain reserved.
**Hard limit**: the planner cannot invoke LF-{NEW} to escape a tight LF-2 second-occupant fit. LF-{NEW} is for genuinely-new shapes, not for LF-2 with one cell flipped.

### Cluster-invariant constraints (non-negotiable for ANY family)
Per CS-LAYOUT-20 — these hold across LF-1..LF-6 and LF-{NEW}. A sibling that proposes deviation triggers a § decision review.

- Serif heading + sans body family · italic-`<em>` emphasis only · no bold · no uppercase headings.
- Cream paper baseline · accent budget ≤3 hits per viewport.
- h1 AAA contrast · dark-section descendant ≥ AA + ΔL ≥120.
- Pexels-only imagery · zero URL overlap across siblings · 3-second subject check.
- Locale-pill switcher · `lang` + `dir` per link · Latin wordmark + numerics in RTL · voice anchor verbatim across 5 locales.
- 100×72 section padding · max-width 1400px.
- Density ceilings (CS-DENSITY-01..07).
- One dark band per home (CS-TONE-03) **or** an explicit family-level demotion declared at planner-brief and ratified at A.6 review-lock (LF-2 is the precedent — do not generalize without the same explicit argument).
- Reduced-motion honoured.
- Editor click-to-edit isolated by `body.mw-is-editor-preview`.
- CTA contract — one primary per viewport · advisor-voice copy · real route hrefs.
- `:focus-visible` gold ring (2px outline + 4px offset).
- Whistleblowing legal-row entry where D.lgs. 24/2023 applies (LF-2 + LF-5 promote it to a footer column).
- ≤720px responsive collapse — hero stacks · nav becomes hamburger drawer · footer collapses.

### Sibling-pair distinctness gate (CS-LAYOUT-12)
Every new sibling must score **≥4/9 layout dimensions different** vs every existing sibling (NOT just vs the closest one). The 6th sibling scores against five live columns now, not three.

## §8 · Maintenance protocol

- **This file is the orchestrator's live reference.** It supersedes `corporate-suite-layout-family-assignment.md` until that file is refreshed (see §6).
- **Each new sibling adds a row to §1 + a column to §7** at intake (declared) and at A.7 walk (rendered). Drift between declared and rendered = CS-LAYOUT-14 fail.
- **Migrations annotate, not overwrite**. A sibling that moves between families keeps both rows · the old row marked `superseded` with a date.
- **The "must NOT repeat" lists in §3 are extended, NOT replaced** — the cluster's prohibition list is monotonic. A new sibling's MUST-NOT list is appended at enrollment close.
- **Pairs in §5 are revisited at every flip**. The Pragma↔Fiscus 2/9 row is overdue for resolution and will be addressed in the next hardening pass.
- **§7 quick-lookup table is the planner's first read**. Keep it current. If a sibling's row drifts (e.g., a re-skin changes a palette token) the table is updated in the same edit pass.

## 3 · Risky inherited / shared patterns (audit before reuse)

These are real artifacts in today's skin that work for the existing five but **must be re-evaluated** before the next sibling ships. They are the load-bearing fault lines.

### R1 · `--primary-2` hardcoded in `_base.html:20`
- Value is `#2c3e6b` (Pragma's navy). Hurts every non-Pragma palette.
- AP7 known issue. CS-PAL-03 prohibits it. Today still in the skin (no regression introduced by Cornice or Continua additions, but the latent debt remains).
- **Action for next sibling**: do not introduce code that reads `--primary-2`. If you need a tinted primary, derive server-side from the three palette tokens.

### R2 · `<720px` hero stack
- AP2 originally · since corrected in the corporate-suite skin per the X.4a hardening cycle (`@media (max-width: 720px) { .cs-hero { grid-template-columns: 1fr; } }`).
- CS-HERO-07 / CS-RESPONSIVE-01 now required as a ship gate.
- **Action for next sibling**: verify the stack works for the chosen L1 hero geometry (split-55-45 stacks naturally · stacked-editorial stays stacked · object-overlay drops the corner credits to inline at ≤720). Each new hero geometry gets its own stack rule.

### R3 · Pragma still on Unsplash imagery (legacy `business-corporate` pool)
- CS-IMG-SRC-01 mandates Pexels. Pragma was grandfathered (corporate_suite.W001 fires on every `sync_template_tiers`).
- **Action for next sibling**: Pexels-only from URL #1. No Unsplash, no AI imagery, no custom photography. CS-IMG-SRC-01 has no exception for new templates. The 5 live pools (`business-corporate` legacy + `business-architecture` + `business-fiscal` + `business-coaching` + `business-stewardship`) cover ~30+ Pexels URLs — the 6th's curator scout time grows linearly but the zero-overlap grep stays automated.

### R4 · `body.mw-is-editor-preview` guard on click-to-edit affordances
- A4 / A5 pattern · `_base.html:441`. Already correct.
- **Action for next sibling**: when copying skin partials, never strip the `body.mw-is-editor-preview` guard from a `:hover` rule. Editor-only affordances must NOT leak into `/templates/<slug>/live/` (CS-MARKET-01).

### R5 · Hero meta-strip composition copies easily into siblinghood collapse
- Five siblings used the meta-strip differently (KPI tuple · KPI in hero overlay · fiscal-calendar · percorso-cadenza · governance-cycle in 2 corner overlays). The shape itself is reusable; the **composition** is the differentiation surface.
- **Action for next sibling**: choose a meta-strip composition that names the firm's PROOF SHAPE (e.g. "iscrizione → giurisdizioni → contenzioso pendenti", "albo → numero pratica → ricorsi vinti", "data fondazione → numero notai → atti rogati", "vincolo → Soprintendenza → cantieri pluriennali"). Do not reuse one of the five existing shapes.

### R6 · `cs-` class prefix is mandatory; Bootstrap classes cannot replace it
- CS-COMP-07 / CS-LAYOUT-21. New components must follow `cs-{name}`.
- **Action for next sibling**: do not import `container-fluid row col-md-4` to avoid writing CSS. The scoped prefix is what keeps the archetype editable in isolation AND what lets the layout-family dispatcher pick the right `_layouts/{lfN}/home.html` shell.

### R7 · Voice anchor copied without the load-bearing italic-em wrap
- F2 / CS-EXEC-01. Anchor lives in `home.html` h1 with `<em>` on the load-bearing word.
- **Action for next sibling**: when authoring the IT anchor, decide which word carries the italic BEFORE the planner sign-off. The word must survive translation (translator brief tells the FR/ES/AR translator which word to italicise post-translation). Solaria's anchor uses TWO em-wraps (`terapia`, `consulenza`); that is acceptable only when the anchor is built on a contrast pair. **Default is one em-wrap, recurring on EXACTLY 2 home surfaces** (LF-2 rule · ratified at Cornice review-lock). LF-5 (Continua) widens the recurrence to 3 surfaces (hero + CTA + footer signature) for the stewardship register.

### R8 · Layout-family declaration must travel from intake → registry → render
- CS-LAYOUT-10 (intake) · CS-LAYOUT-22 (registry `layout_family` column) · CS-LAYOUT-14 (render matches declaration). The dispatcher in `_layouts/{lf1..lf5}/home.html` reads the registry field.
- **Action for next sibling**: planner declares LF-{N} at intake; builder sets the `layout_family` field in the seed; the renderer dispatches to the right `_layouts/lfN/home.html`. If the field is missing the renderer falls through to LF-1 silently. The 6th sibling cannot rely on the planner's reliability — verify the field is set at A.5 build before A.6 critique.

### R9 · 4-col-with-whistleblowing footer is shared between LF-2 and LF-5
- LF-2 (Cornice) and LF-5 (Continua) both ship a 4-col-with-whistleblowing footer. The shape is shared by intent (D.lgs. 24/2023 compliance posture); the column content is sub-cluster-specific.
- **Action for next sibling**: a 7th sibling that picks LF-2 (second occupant) inherits the 4-col-with-whistleblowing footer verbatim. A non-LF-2/non-LF-5 sibling that wants whistleblowing as a column must justify it explicitly at planner-brief and ratify it at A.6.

### R10 · Cornice's CS-TONE-03 demotion is LF-2-scoped, NOT a portable precedent
- LF-2 (Cornice) ships zero dark bands on home (KPI in hero overlay + cream CTA closer with hairline rust borders). This is family-scoped per CS-LAYOUT-05 and ratified at Cornice A.6 review-lock.
- **Action for next sibling**: do NOT invoke "Cornice set the precedent" to demote CS-TONE-03 in a non-LF-2 family. The demotion is family-scoped, not cluster-scoped. The orchestrator gates this at planner-brief sign-off.

- **Continua (`business-stewardship` · Pexels)**: library reading-room interior (hero · zero people · interior-warm-mahogany · horizontal partner-desk) · vault / archive shelves (feature) · 3 environmental portraits at vault / desk / boardroom (slots 2-4) · monochrome iconography for the 4-pillar matrix (slot 5). **Mood**: warm-mahogany monumental · object-led · zero-people-in-hero. **Subject density**: zero in hero · 3 distributed environmental portraits.

### Rhythm rules the cluster follows
1. **Slot 0 (hero) sets the entire mood.** It is THE photo a first-time visitor remembers. Treat it as the brand decision — choose it before copy. **Two of five now ship zero-people heroes (Cornice exterior · Continua interior)** — the office-shot monoculture broke at the LF-2 + LF-5 enrollments.
2. **Slot 1 (feature) is the "wide context" shot.** Used on `about.html` hero and as second-section accent on home. Reads as institutional environment.
3. **Slots 2-3 (portraits)** are square-ish (≥800×800), match the firm's actual age/gender mix, NEVER use the gray-silhouette placeholder, NEVER reuse slot 0. For LF-5 (Continua) the 3 portraits distribute across vault/desk/boardroom rather than stacking.
4. **Slot 4 (detail)** is the "what we do" close-up — documents, instruments, artifacts. Replaces "fake handshake" stock with semantic proof.
5. **Slot 5 (ambient)** is the "where we work" environmental shot — bookshelf, atrium, plant-lit corner. Reads as place. **LF-5 substitutes monochrome iconography for the 4-pillar matrix at this slot.**
6. **Zero URL overlap across siblings** (CS-IMG-SRC-04). The grep is automated. Five live pools cover ~30+ Pexels URLs · the union grows linearly per new sibling.

### Risks observed (don't repeat)
- Pragma's slot 5 conference detail blurs into Fiscus's institutional bookshelf if the next sibling re-uses bookshelf semantics. **Pick a non-bookshelf ambient.**
- Solaria's slot 2 (woman with clipboard) and slot 3 (businesswoman arms-crossed) are both 30-something Caucasian; the next portrait-bearing sibling needs visible age/gender/ethnicity diversity OR a different framing entirely (3/4 profile, side light, environmental portrait — Cornice and Continua both demonstrated environmental).
- Cornice + Continua both ship zero-people heroes. They do not collide on subject (exterior architectural vs interior archive) or temperature (golden-hour exterior stone vs mahogany interior) but a 6th zero-people hero must triangulate against both.
- Three of the five (Pragma + Fiscus + Solaria) still ship interior-office scenes — a 6th interior-office reads as "the cluster is mostly office shots." Prefer object-led / exterior-architectural / library-archive / atelier-workshop / conservation-laboratory directions.

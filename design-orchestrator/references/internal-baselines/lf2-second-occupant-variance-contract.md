## §4 · The five rules (binding form · same as hardening report)

### AC-V1 [REQUIRED] · Within-cell sub-variant adoption ≥ 3
A 2nd LF-2 occupant adopts ≥3 patterns from the LF-2 sub-variant pool that
the 1st occupant (Cornice) does not ship: NAV-1 · NAV-3 · EDIT-2 · QUOTE-4 ·
EVID-2 · EVID-3 · EVID-5 · TIME-3 · KPI-2.

A 3rd LF-2 occupant adopts ≥3 from the same pool AND ≥1 of those must be
unused by both Cornice (0 ships) AND the 2nd occupant (Causa ships 5
post-retrofit · the open set for the 3rd is NAV-3 · EDIT-2 · QUOTE-4 ·
EVID-2).

### AC-V2 [REQUIRED] · Portrait composition divergence ≥ 2 axes
The 2nd occupant's leadership single-portrait composition diverges ≥2 of:
posture · activity · room props · photographic register, vs Cornice (and
vs Causa for the 3rd occupant).

### AC-V3 [REQUIRED] · CTA inflection family divergence
The 2nd occupant's CTA inflection family is non-`Apri-X`. The 3rd's is
non-Apri-X AND non-(Causa's locked class). Cross-family verb-classes
(`Fissa-X` Pragma · `Primo + abstract noun` Fiscus · `Prenota-X` Solaria ·
`Avvia-X` Continua) are also forbidden inside LF-2.

### AC-V4 [REQUIRED] · KPI cell semantic-class divergence ≥ 1 cell
The 2nd occupant's hero-overlay KPI tuple shares the 3-cell SHAPE with
Cornice (per AC-1 verbatim L5 inheritance) but diverges semantic class on
≥1 of the 3 cells. The 3rd occupant diverges semantic class on ≥1 cell vs
Cornice's tuple AND ≥1 cell vs the 2nd occupant's locked tuple.

### AC-V5 [REQUIRED] · Cumulative two-pair ladder for 3rd occupant
The 3rd LF-2 occupant clears AC-V1..V4 vs **both** Cornice AND the 2nd
occupant. Two-pair scoring at A.1 intake; both pairs must clear 27/54
within-family threshold AND all 5 critical-axis vetoes ≥ floor.

(The ratification reasoning, score-raise contributions, and failure modes
are in `factory/reports/hardening/lf2-family-internal-variance-rules.md
§4`. This contract is the orchestrator-side condensed binding form.)

## §5 · How the planner uses this contract at A.1 intake

The planner's A.1 brief for an LF-2 second/third occupant carries:

1. **Prerequisite check**: P1-P7 answered. Any "no" → contract does not open.
2. **AC-V1 sub-variant declaration**: list ≥3 chosen sub-variants from the
   pool. For the 3rd occupant, ≥1 must be unused by both Cornice and the
   2nd occupant.
3. **AC-V2 portrait composition declaration**: name the 4 axes (posture ·
   activity · room props · photographic register). Confirm ≥2 differ vs
   each existing LF-2 occupant.
4. **AC-V3 CTA verb-class declaration**: name the chosen class (Sottometti-X
   · Richiedi-X · Visualizza-X · Esplora-X · etc.). Confirm not in Cornice's
   `Apri-X` AND not in the 2nd occupant's locked class for the 3rd.
5. **AC-V4 KPI tuple semantic shape declaration**: write the 3-cell tuple
   with semantic-class label per cell (count · period · publication ·
   achievement · year-range). Confirm ≥1 cell diverges class vs each
   existing occupant.
6. **18-axis matrix**: scored vs each existing sibling using `visible-
   distinctness-thresholds-v2.md §2` rubric. Cross-family pairs ≥36 ·
   near-occupant pairs ≥30 · within-family pairs ≥27. All 5 critical-axis
   vetoes ≥ floor.
7. **F2-VAR-1..12 walk plan**: the planner's brief names which gates fire
   for this occupant (all 12 for a 3rd · gates 1-6 + 8-11 for the 2nd ·
   gate 7 fires only for 3rd-vs-Causa pair).
8. **Hard limits** from `cornice-lf2-reference-pack.md §4 AC-1..AC-16`
   re-stated explicitly in the brief.

If any of (2) (3) (4) (6) fails, the brief returns to A.0 territory-scout.

## §8 · Maintenance protocol

- A new LF-2 occupant intake adds a column to §3 (claims registry · the
  occupant's locked values) AT A.6 LOCK and AT public flip.

  X.7b motion_profile DNA OR future cluster ships) extends the AC-V1 pool.
  Existing LF-2 occupants are NOT retroactively required to adopt the new
  sub-variants.
- Per-family AC-V rule sets for LF-1 / LF-3 / LF-4 / LF-5 are paper-deferred
  until a 2nd-occupant intake proposes the family. LF-6 will author its
  set when its 1st occupant ships.
- F2-VAR-1..12 browser gates are codified in `factory/reports/hardening/
  lf2-family-internal-variance-rules.md §6` and applied at workflow C/D
  walks for any LF-2 occupant after the 1st.
- The 27/54 within-family threshold (per `anti-clone-2.0-rules.md §3`) is
  the binding output gate; this contract's AC-V1..V5 are the binding input
  gates that, when cleared, raise the score into the threshold range.

## §0 · Why this file exists

The anti-clone 2.0 audit (`anti-clone-2.0-rules.md`) scored Cornice ↔ Causa
at **21/54 · 39%** with three critical-axis vetoes failed:

- axis 13 CTA mental model + inflection family · score 1 ✗
- axis 17 imagery register · score 1 ✗
- axis 18 motion gravity + page choreography · score 0 ✗

The audit's verdict was unambiguous: the pair is too-related, retrofit
required, but **family migration is the wrong fix**. Causa belongs in LF-2
by correct family-fit — portfolio-of-work-led firm whose proof IS its case
bundle (per `corporate-suite-live-family-map.md §4 · LF-2 second occupant`).
Migrating Causa to LF-{NEW} would force a 13-doc family declaration, lose
LF-2's editorial-publication register fit, and reset the LF-2 second-occupant
precedent.

The correct fix is **LF-2 internal variance rules**: positive rules that bind
a 2nd (and future 3rd) LF-2 occupant to within-cell diversification across
the cells that critical-axis vetoes touch. Five rules — AC-V1, AC-V2, AC-V3,
AC-V4, AC-V5 — were paper-proposed at `corporate-suite-retrofit-priority-plan.md
§1`. This file ratifies them.

After ratification, the LF-2 family carries:

| **LF-2 family inheritance** | AC-1..AC-16 (verbatim L1–L9 inheritance · what the 1st occupant locks) | `cornice-lf2-reference-pack.md §4` |

is the LF-2 row of the premium-dynamic-pattern catalog (`premium-dynamic-pattern-library.md
§2 · Cluster fit: corporate-suite LF-2`).

library — and Cornice's baseline ship-state:

A 2nd LF-2 occupant ships **≥ 3** of NAV-1 · NAV-3 · EDIT-2 · QUOTE-4 · EVID-2 ·
EVID-3 · EVID-5 · TIME-3 · KPI-2.

**Failure mode**: a 2nd LF-2 occupant lands `tier=draft` shipping the static
Cornice baseline + 0-2 sub-variants. Catch at A.5 build (the builder's brief
declares the chosen sub-variants) AND at A.6 review-lock (the style-critic

cells (EVID-3 + EVID-5) ALSO raises axis 9 (cases-preview) and axis 17

### AC-V5 [REQUIRED] · Cumulative ladder for 3rd LF-2 occupant

A 3rd LF-2 occupant — if proposed for sibling 7 or any later intake — MUST
clear AC-V1, AC-V2, AC-V3, AC-V4 vs **both** the 1st (Cornice) AND the
2nd (Causa) occupants. The ladder is cumulative, not maximal-pair-only.

Mechanically:
- **AC-V1**: ≥3 sub-variants adopted AND at least 1 sub-variant unused by
  both Cornice (ships 0) AND Causa (ships R2+R3+R4+R5+R6 = EVID-3 +
  TIME-3 + NAV-1 + KPI-2 + EVID-5 · so the open set for the 3rd is
  NAV-3 · EDIT-2 · QUOTE-4 · EVID-2).
- **AC-V2**: portrait composition diverges ≥2 axes vs Cornice AND ≥2 axes
  vs Causa.
- **AC-V3**: CTA verb-class differs from both Cornice (`Apri-X`) AND
  Causa's chosen class · the open set is whatever Causa does NOT land on.
- **AC-V4**: KPI cell semantic-shape diverges ≥1 cell vs Cornice's tuple
  AND ≥1 cell vs Causa's tuple.

The 3rd-occupant intake at workflow A.1 carries an explicit two-pair scoring
matrix (3rd vs Cornice on all 18 axes · 3rd vs Causa on all 18 axes) per
`anti-clone-2.0-rules.md §10`. **Both** pair scores must clear 27/54
within-family threshold AND **both** must clear all 5 critical-axis vetoes.

**Promoted to GUIDELINE → REQUIRED**: this rule was paper-proposed at
`corporate-suite-retrofit-priority-plan.md §1 · "AC-V5 [GUIDELINE]"`. This
file ratifies it as `[REQUIRED]` because a single soft "guideline" is
insufficient for a 3rd-occupant intake — without the cumulative ladder
binding both pairs, the 3rd occupant can score against Cornice or Causa
selectively and pass while still collapsing visibly against the other.

**Failure mode**: a 3rd LF-2 occupant lands at A.5 build with the matrix
filled vs only 1 of {Cornice, Causa}. Catch at A.1 intake (planner brief
must carry both columns).

LF-2 cells (single-portrait at L6 · hero-overlay KPI at L5 · magazine-grid at

### F2-VAR-1 [BLOCKING] · Sub-variant adoption count
- **What**: count rendered LF-2 sub-variants on the new occupant's home.
- **How**: at workflow C walk, the verifier emulates 1280×800 default-motion
  AND 1280×800 `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` AND probes:

    static at reduced-motion).

family choice.** Both siblings belong in LF-2 by correct family-fit (their
